Acrylic Flow Paintings

Acrylic Pouring is not just a fun and popular flow painting technique, but creating from the energy of flow is also a powerful state of being to explore and embody. In the process of pouring paints, inks, water, and pouring mediums onto canvas or other objects, the real magic comes alive without the control and manipulation necessary when using brushes or other conventional artist tools. In many ways it becomes less about a specific talent or technique, and more about a trust and commitment to what’s emerging. Newbies often get great results because they’re having so much fun. They try new recipes, experiment with techniques, all the while wondering, what if I did this, added that, or let go of the rules others are playing by? The creative process is pure magic when we leave the confines of critical mind chatter and flow from the soul’s calling.
